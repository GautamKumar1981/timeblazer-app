# Timeblazer User Guide

## Getting Started

1. Open the app and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your name, email, and password
3. Set your timezone and workday hours in Settings
4. You're ready to plan your first day!

---

## Core Methodology

Timeblazer is built on **timeboxing** — scheduling every task into a dedicated time slot *before* your day begins. Unlike a to-do list, a timebox answers: "When exactly will I do this?" This creates commitment, reduces decision fatigue, and makes your time visible.

The daily rhythm:
1. **Plan** the night before or first thing in the morning
2. **Execute** each timebox, using Focus Mode to stay on track
3. **Review** at end of day to learn and improve

---

## Daily Workflow

### Morning: Set Top 3 Priorities
Before creating timeboxes, identify your 3 most important tasks for the day. These are the non-negotiables — if nothing else gets done, these must.

### Create Timeboxes
Drag on the calendar to create a time block, or click **+ New Timebox**. Set:
- Title and category
- Start and end time
- Color for visual grouping
- Optional description/notes

### Execute with Focus Mode
Click **Focus** on any timebox to enter full-screen mode with a countdown timer. All distractions are hidden.

### End-of-Day Review
Mark timeboxes complete or skipped. Add notes on what went well and what to improve.

## Features Guide

### Timeboxing
- **Create:** Click `+` or drag on the calendar grid
- **Edit:** Click any timebox to open the detail panel
- **Color-code:** Use colors to distinguish Work, Personal, Health, etc.
- **Move:** Drag and drop to reschedule

### Calendar Views
- **Day view:** Hourly grid for today
- **Week view:** Overview of the full week
- **Month view:** High-level pattern view
- Navigate with arrow keys or the date picker

### Focus Mode
- Press `F` or click **Focus** on a timebox
- Full-screen countdown timer
- **Parking Lot** panel to capture interruptions without losing focus
- Press `Esc` to exit, `Space` to pause

### Goals & D-Day Countdown
- Create goals with a target deadline
- A countdown widget shows days remaining
- Link timeboxes to goals for progress tracking

### Analytics
- **Completion Rate:** % of timeboxes completed vs. planned
- **Category Breakdown:** Pie chart of time by category
- **Daily Trends:** Bar chart of daily completion over time
- **Streak:** Consecutive days with at least one completed timebox

### Weekly Review
- Available every Sunday (or manually triggered)
- Auto-generated summary of the week's stats
- Reflection prompts: wins, challenges, improvements
- Saved reviews are searchable

### Streak Tracking
- Earn a streak day by completing at least one timebox
- Current and longest streak displayed on the dashboard
- Streak resets if you miss a day

## Tips & Best Practices

- **Add buffer time** (10–15 min) between tasks for transitions
- **90-minute deep work blocks** are ideal for complex tasks
- **Pomodoro technique:** Use 25-minute timeboxes with 5-minute breaks
- **Parking Lot:** Write down interruptions immediately, deal with them later
- **Weekly Review:** Do it every Sunday to close the loop and plan ahead
- **Start small:** Timebox only 3–4 hours your first week, then expand
- **Review yesterday** before planning today to carry over unfinished work
