# Timeblazer User Guide

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Open Timeblazer at http://localhost:3000
2. Click **Register** on the login page
3. Enter your email, username, and password
4. Click **Create Account**

### Logging In

1. Enter your email and password
2. Click **Login**
3. You'll be redirected to the Dashboard

---

## Dashboard

The Dashboard shows your day at a glance:
- **Today's Timeboxes**: All scheduled time blocks for today
- **Top Priorities**: Your 3 most important tasks for the day
- **Quick Stats**: Completed vs total timeboxes

---

## Timeboxes

Timeboxing is the core feature of Timeblazer. Each timebox represents a dedicated block of time for a specific task.

### Creating a Timebox

1. Go to **Calendar** page
2. Click on a time slot or click **New Timebox**
3. Fill in:
   - Title (required)
   - Start/end time
   - Category (work, personal, health, learning)
   - Priority (1-5)
   - Color

### Completing a Timebox

1. Find your timebox on the Dashboard or Calendar
2. Click the **✓ Complete** button
3. The timebox will be marked as complete and analytics updated

---

## Goals

### Creating a Goal

1. Go to **Goals** page
2. Click **+ New Goal**
3. Enter title, description, target date, and category
4. Set initial progress (0-100%)

### Tracking Progress

- Update the progress percentage as you make progress
- The D-Day countdown shows how many days until the target date
- Goals can be marked as Completed or Archived

---

## Focus Mode

Focus Mode provides a distraction-free timer experience:

1. Click **Focus** in the navigation
2. Select an active timebox
3. Click **Start Timer**
4. Work until the timer ends!

Controls:
- **Start/Pause**: Toggle the timer
- **Reset**: Reset to full duration
- **Stop**: End the focus session

---

## Analytics

The Analytics page shows your productivity data:

- **Productivity Score**: Based on timebox completion rate and focus time
- **Weekly Chart**: Daily productivity scores over the past week
- **Focus Time**: Total minutes spent in focused work

---

## Weekly Review

Complete a weekly review each Friday to reflect on your week:

1. Go to **Weekly Review** page
2. Click **New Review**
3. Fill in:
   - **Accomplishments**: What did you achieve this week?
   - **Challenges**: What was difficult?
   - **Goals for Next Week**: What do you want to accomplish?
   - **Overall Rating**: Rate your week 1-5

## Tips for Success

1. **Plan your day the night before** - Set tomorrow's timeboxes before bed
2. **Set 3 daily priorities** - Focus on the most important tasks
3. **Use the Focus Mode** - Minimize distractions during timeboxes
4. **Complete weekly reviews** - Reflection improves future planning
5. **Track your goals** - Regular progress updates keep you motivated
